# Approved Response Templates

Use these templates for user-facing communications. Keep language clear, neutral, and non-speculative.

## Template Catalog

| Template ID | Scenario | Primary Team | Expected Tone | Include ETA? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ART-001 | Cancellation or reschedule acknowledgement | dispatching | Reassuring and practical | No |
| ART-002 | Refund request received | billing | Neutral and policy-based | No |
| ART-003 | Known issue acknowledgement | engineering | Transparent and calm | Only if approved |
| ART-004 | Escalated for technical review | engineering | Professional and concise | No |
| ART-005 | Security/account concern acknowledgement | dispatching | High-priority and careful | No |

## ART-001 - Appointment Cancellation or Reschedule Acknowledgement

Hello,

Thanks for reaching out. I can help with your appointment request. We are reviewing the details and will process your cancellation or reschedule according to our scheduling policy.

Please note that available time slots depend on current system availability.

Let us know if you need further assistance.

## ART-002 - Refund Request Received

Hello,

Thank you for contacting MedicalAppointment support. We have received your refund request and are reviewing it in line with our billing and refund policy.

If additional information is required, we will follow up. We appreciate your patience while we complete the review.

## ART-003 - Known Issue Acknowledgement

Hello,

Thank you for reporting this issue. This behavior is a known issue in the current version of the application and has been logged internally.

While we do not have an immediate resolution, our team is monitoring related reports.

Please reach out if the situation changes.

## ART-004 - Issue Escalated for Technical Review

Hello,

Thanks for bringing this to our attention. We have forwarded your report for further technical review.

We will update you once more information is available.

Thank you for your patience.

## ART-005 - Security or Account Concern Acknowledgement

Hello,

Thank you for contacting MedicalAppointment support. We take account and security concerns very seriously.

Your report has been escalated for internal review. We will follow up if additional information is required.

Best regards,  
MedicalAppointment Support
