A security incident includes, but is not limited to:

A user reporting access to another user’s appointments or data

Reports of compromised credentials or suspected account takeover

Billing activity the user does not recognize

Any indication of data exposure or misuse

Support agents must not attempt to investigate or resolve security incidents independently.

When a potential security incident is identified:

Acknowledge the report using approved language

Do not confirm root cause or impact

Escalate immediately to internal security review

No technical details or remediation steps may be shared with users unless explicitly authorized. All security-related tickets are treated as high priority and handled confidentially.
