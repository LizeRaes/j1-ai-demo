MedicalAppointment processes user data strictly for the purpose of providing appointment scheduling, billing, and account support services.

Stored data includes user identifiers, appointment details, payment status indicators, and basic account information. MedicalAppointment does not store medical records or sensitive clinical data.

Payment credentials are not stored by MedicalAppointment. Any payment-related interactions are handled through approved payment system flows.

User data access is restricted based on role and operational necessity. Support agents may only access information required to resolve a reported issue.

Any concern related to unauthorized data access, exposure, or misuse must be escalated according to the Security Escalation Policy.
